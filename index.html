About this event

SpringFest Ogbomosho is the largest Developer Group event happening in the City of Ogbomosho, where we host thousands of developers on a day activity-packed event focused on technical learning, shared passion for products, and networking with like minds.

It is a series of community-led events where you can learn the essentials and best practices of various technologies and products, such as Android, Web, Google Cloud Platform, and more. This event promises to be bigger, and better.

And best of all, attendance is free!
